About the Project

This platform is built to detect manipulated voice and face content using audio and video models. It is designed for practical deployment: upload handling, prediction persistence, user feedback loops, and clean reporting in one place.

The system analyzes uploaded media, extracts meaningful features, and returns deepfake probability through model voting and acoustic analysis. Feedback can be sent directly after prediction to improve future training data.

With support for cached responses and duplicate detection, the app delivers faster repeated results while keeping an auditable history in the database layer.
